class: glass

attributes:
material (string)
maximumCapacity (integer)
isClear (boolean)
color (array)

methods:
make_glass (turns glass material to glass)
Example: make_glass: material: "glass"

half_full (divides maximumCapacity by 2)
var maximumCapacity = 10
Example: half_full: maximumCapacity: 5

pour_beer (add beer to glass)
Example: pour_beer: isClear: false

make_black (changes color to black)
example: make_black: color: ["black"]
